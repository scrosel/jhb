SEASONS AND EPISODES



During the course of the series, 160 episodes of Steven Universe aired over five seasons, between November 4, 2013, and January 21, 2019.

A television film, Steven Universe: The Movie, was released on September 2, 2019, following the fifth and final season.



SEASON 1


Episode 1 - Gem Glow

- After Steven's gem activates, the Crystal Gems attempt to teach him how to summon his weapon; meanwhile, a monster attacks.


Episode 2 - Laser Light Canon

- Steven and his father use a weapon left behind by Rose Quartz which saves Beach City from destruction.


Episode 3 - Cheeseburger Backpack

- Steven attempts to prove his usefulness on a mission.


Episode 4 - Together Breakfast

- Steven is taken throughout the Temple while trying to share a breakfast he made for himself and the other Gems.


Episode 5 - Frybo

- Steven accidentally causes a mascot costume to come to life with the use of Gem Shards.


Episode 6 - Cat Fingers

- Steven attempts to shapeshift into a cat but loses control.


Episode 7 - Bubble Buddies

- Steven and his new friend Connie get trapped in Steven's bubble.


Episode 8 - Serious Steven

- The Gems take Steven on his first serious mission.


Episode 9 - Tiger Millionaire

- Steven and Amethyst sneak out every night to pursue their new wrestling careers.


Episode 10 - Steven's Lion

- Steven finds a magical pink Lion in the desert that follows him home.


Episode 11 - Arcade Mania

- In celebration of a successful mission, Steven takes the Gems to the Funland Arcade, where Garnet becomes addicted to a game.


Episode 12 - Giant Woman

- After hearing about fusion, Steven continuously asks Pearl and Amethyst to fuse into Opal while on their mission at the Sky Spire.


Episode 13 - So Many Birthdays

- Steven throws a birthday party for each of the Gems, and then questions whether or not he is too old for parties, activating a new power.


Episode 14 - Lars and the Cool Kids

- Steven joins Lars in hanging out with the Cool Kids, and saves the day when Rose's Moss engulfs everyone.


Episode 15 - Onion Trade

- Steven trades Onion a dangerous weapon in return for one of his favorite toys.


Episode 16 - Steven the Sword Fighter

- Upon request, Pearl attempts to teach Steven the art of swordfighting, but is stabbed by one of her holograms, teaching Steven what Gem poofing is.


Episode 17 - Lion 2: The Movie

- Lion takes Steven and Connie to Rose's Secret Armory, where they take a look at many of Rose's weapons.


Episode 18 - Beach Party

- Steven throws a beach party for the Gems and Pizza family to reconcile after Garnet accidentally breaks their shop sign and gets them banned.


Episode 19 - Rose's Room

- Wishing to be alone, Steven enters Rose's Room, where the room recreates a realistic Beach City in an attempt to give Steven what he wants.


Episode 20 - Coach Steven

- After Sugilite becomes unstable at the Communication Hub, Steven encourages Pearl to defeat her when she returns to the beach.


pisode 21 - Joking Victim

- Steven helps Sadie get back at Lars for humiliating her.


Episode 22 - Steven and the Stevens

- Steven tries to fix a problem by going back in time using the Hourglass, but causes the timelines to go berserk.


Episode 23 - Monster Buddies

- Steven tames Centipeetle after he accidentally unbubbles her.


Episode 24 - An Indirect Kiss

- Steven tells Connie the story of how he tried using his healing powers to fix Amethyst's cracked gem.


Episode 25 - Mirror Gem

- When Pearl gives Steven a magical mirror, Steven discovers that Lapis is trapped inside.


Episode 26 - Ocean Gem

- When the ocean suddenly disappears, Steven and the Gems travel to Lapis Lazuli's water tower to get it back.


Episode 27 - House Guest

- Greg stays at the Temple with his "broken leg", and constantly uses the Warp Whistle to bring Steven back from his mission.


Episode 28 - Space Race

- With Pearl's fascination of space back, Steven, Pearl, and Greg travel to Greg's family barn to build a spaceship.


Episode 29 - Secret Team

- Steven, Amethyst, and Pearl form a team to retrieve missing Gem Shards that have scattered throughout the Temple with the hopes of not alerting Garnet.


Episode 30 - Island Adventure

- Steven takes Lars and Sadie to Mask Island for a vacation, but become stranded when the Warp Pad mysteriously disappears.


Episode 31 - Keep Beach City Weird

- Steven joins Ronaldo uncover the weirdness in Beach City, but realizes that the Crystal Gems caused all traces of weirdness.


Episode 32 - Fusion Cuisine

- Steven has the Alexandrite act as his mother at a dinner with Connie's family.


Episode 33 - Garnet's Universe

- Steven imagines what Garnet's solo missions are like.


Episode 34 - Watermelon Steven

- After accidentally turning watermelons into sentient soldiers with a new power, Steven tries to stop the Watermelon Stevens from causing chaos in Beach City.


Episode 35 - Lion 3: Straight to Video

- Lion will not stop laying on Steven as the latter sleeps while experiencing abnormal dreams. However, this leads to Steven discovering Lion's Dimension and a VHS tape made by Greg Universe and Rose Quartz.


Episode 36 - Warp Tour

- Steven witnesses something else flying through the warp tunnels and tries to get to the bottom of it while everyone tries to ease his paranoia. It is then discovered that Peridot was utilizing robonoids to fix Warp Pads.


Episode 37 - Alone Together

- The Crystal Gems teach Steven how to fuse. After the lesson, Steven and Connie fuse accidentally and begin to experience it together.


Episode 38 - The Test

- Steven discovers the events in "Cheeseburger Backpack" were a test and not a real mission. Steven demands an objective that presents real danger, much to the Crystal Gems' reluctance.


Episode 39 - Future Vision

- Steven experiences Garnet's future vision for the first time, but having the foresight to see various potential deathtraps causes Steven to become paranoid to the point of insanity.


Episode 40 - On the Run

- Steven and Amethyst explore the outside world further, ending up at the Kindergarten where battle goes down.


Episode 41 - Horror Club

- It is horror night for Steven and his peers, getting ready to watch some spooky motion pictures.


Episode 42 - Winter Forecast

- A large snowstorm is incoming, prompting Connie to head back home before it comes. The objective is not as simple as it seems.


Episode 43 - Maximum Capacity

- "Lil' Butler" comes back after years of isolation from Greg and Amethyst's attention. The two become addicted to the TV series, and that delays their work.


Episode 44 - Marble Madness

- Multiple robonoids start to appear, and the Crystal Gems decide to track down their designated location to stop the neverending arrivals.


Episode 45 - Rose's Scabbard

- Pearl goes on a mission with Steven that turns into a battle of knowing secrets.


Episode 46 - Open Book

- Steven and Connie attempt to roleplay the latter's favorite book series in Rose's room, only to meet complications with the room itself.


Episode 47 - Shirt Club

- Buck Dewey and Steven make T-shirts together, but Buck gets carried away with the design presented by Steven.


Episode 48 - Story for Steven

- Greg tells Steven the story of him and Rose Quartz, the story containing drama, first sights, and hard decisions being made.


Episode 49 - The Message

- The wailing stone keeps making an annoying sound because it cannot interpret the message that is trying to be received. The Crystal Gems and Greg attempt to solve it.


Episode 50 - Political Power

- Outrage happens in Beach City from an electrical blackout.


Episode 51 - The Return

- A giant hand appears in the sky, and the Crystal Gems are greeted from said spaceship by Homeworld Gems.


Episode 52 - Jail Break

- The Crystal Gems escape the Gem Warship and fight off the Homeworld opposition.
