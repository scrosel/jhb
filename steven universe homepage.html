STEVEN UNIVERSE

by Rebbecca Sugar

The Crystal Gems are a team of magical beings who are the self-appointed guardians of the universe. Steven Universe, a half human, half gem, is the youngest member of the group.
Even though he's not as powerful, or smart as fellow group members Garnet, Amethyst and Pearl, he learns to use the magical powers that come from his bellybutton and goes on magical adventures with the rest of the Crystal Gems.



Genre: Action; Science fantasy; Comedy drama; Coming of age;
